Why Tea?

At Somerville Tea Haven, we're more than just tea enthusiasts; we're tea advocates. Our mission is to share the remarkable advantages of tea while honoring its centuries-old legacy. A Heritage Steeped in Tradition Tea's story spans over 5,000 years, originating in ancient China. Legend has it that Emperor Shen Nong's chance encounter with tea leaves in a pot of boiling water marked the beginning of tea's journey. Over time, tea became a global treasure, transcending borders and weaving a rich tapestry of cultures. Embracing the Health Benefits At Somerville Tea Haven, we're passionate about the tea offers:

  • Antioxidant Boost:

    Tea is a potent source of antioxidants that protect cells and DNA.

  • Heart Health:

    Enjoy a lower risk of heart disease with tea's help in maintaining healthy cholesterol and blood pressure levels.

  • Weight Wellness:

    Green tea's catechins boost metabolism and promote weight management.

  • Mental Clarity:

    L-theanine in tea enhances focus and cognitive function.

  • Digestive Comfort:

    Herbal teas like peppermint and ginger soothe digestion.

  • Immune Support:

    Polyphenols in tea help strengthen the immune system.

  • Hydration:

    Stay refreshed with tea, a healthier alternative to sugary drinks.
